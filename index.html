State Health IT Privacy and Consent Laws and Policies

Welcome! This website provides state laws and policies that regulate patient consent for sharing their personal health information, specifically related to mental health. The information provided covers state-level policies and laws related to Health Information Exchange (HIE) consent, authorization requirements for the disclosure of mental health information for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations (TPO), and minimum necessary standards for the disclosure of mental health information. The website also provides the laws and policies for the District of Columbia.
